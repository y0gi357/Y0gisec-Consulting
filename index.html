Frequently Asked Questions

What is penetration testing?

Penetration testing, or pen testing, is a simulated cyberattack against your computer system to check for exploitable vulnerabilities. It's a crucial part of a comprehensive security strategy.

How often should I conduct security audits?

The frequency of security audits depends on several factors, including industry regulations, changes in your IT infrastructure, and the sensitivity of your data. Generally, annual audits are recommended, with more frequent assessments for high-risk environments or after significant system changes.

What is the difference between vulnerability scanning and penetration testing?

Vulnerability scanning uses automated tools to identify known vulnerabilities, providing a broad overview. Penetration testing, on the other hand, is a manual process that attempts to exploit identified vulnerabilities to determine the actual risk and potential impact of a successful attack.

Do you offer incident response services?

Yes, Y0giSec Consulting provides comprehensive incident response services. Our team can help you prepare for, detect, contain, eradicate, recover from, and learn from cybersecurity incidents to minimize damage and downtime.
